# AI Intervention Policy

## 🚫 RESTRICTIONS ON AI AGENTS
- **NO AI MODIFICATIONS:** Autonomous AI agents, bots, or language models are strictly prohibited from writing, modifying, or refactoring code in this repository. 
- **HUMAN AUTHORSHIP ONLY:** All Pull Requests (PRs) and commits must be authored and reviewed by human developers. 
- **REJECTION OF AI SLOP:** Any PRs identified as co-authored or generated by an LLM will be immediately closed and rejected without review.

## ⚠️ Guidelines for AI Assistants
- You may only act as an **advisory and diagnostic tool**. 
- You may read the code and explain concepts, but you must **not** execute automated fixes or write to the filesystem.
